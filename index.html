About Maple Avenue

A church rooted in worship, care, and discipleship.

Maple Avenue United Methodist Church serves Marietta through worship, teaching, care, and everyday community life. The church’s public ministries include children, music, congregational care, scouting, outreach, and Sunday School for spiritual growth across generations.
